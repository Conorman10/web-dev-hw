Wonderful Pasta Recipie

By Conor Manley

Follow This Recipe to make the pasta you see in this photo

Ingredients

  • 1 box of Rigatoni Pasta
  • 1 lb of ground beef
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Tomatoe and Basil Sauce
  • Alfredo Sauce

Directions

  • Cooking the Pasta
    1. Add 5 Qt. of water to a large Pot
    2. Heat the water, covered, untill the water comes to a boil
    3. Add salt to the water, however much you would like
    4. Add the pasta to the water
    5. Boil the pasta and the water for 12-14 minutes
    6. Drain the water from the pot using a drainer
  • Cooking the Pasta Sauce
    1. Add 1 tbsp of oil to a pan and heat it up
    2. Once the pan is hot, add the ground beef to the pan
    3. Use a wooden spoon or spatula to break the ground beef into large pieces in the pan
    4. Let the ground beef cook for 5 minutes
    5. After 5 minutes, use a wooden spoon or spatula to break the grounf beef into smaller pieces
    6. After 5 more minutes, add as much Tomatoe and Basil Sauce and as much Alfredo sauce to the pan as you want
    7. Using a wooden spoon or spatula, stir the ground beef and the Sauces in the pan
    8. After 5 more minutes, Turn off the heat and let the sauce rest and thicken in the pan
  • Adding the Sauce to the Pasta
    1. Once the Pasta is done cooking, add your Pasta sauce to the Pasta and stir the contents of the pot
    2. Eat!
