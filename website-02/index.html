HOW TO MAKE THE BEST LASAGNA EVER



Step 1 - Gather Your Materials:

  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 2 Bell Peppers
  • 1 onion
  • 1 Jar of Tomato and Basil Sauce
  • 3 Tomatoes
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 Pack of Lasagna Noodles
  • Oil
  • 1 Large Pot
  • 1 Large Pan
  • 1 Spatula
  • 1 Knife
  • 1 Cutting Board

Step 2 - Create the Sauce:

  1. Add Oil to the pan and heat it up for 2 minutes
  2. Add Ground Beef to the Pan and crush it into small pieces with the spatula
  3. Cook the Ground Beef for 5 minutes, seasoning the beef however you's like
  4. While the Beef is cooking, Cut up the vegetables into small pieces
  5. One the Beef has cooked for 5 minutes, add the vegetables to the Pan
  6. Add the tomatoes to the Pan and crush them into a paste with the spatula
  7. Cook the Ground Beef & Vegetables for 5-10 more minutes, stirring frequently
  8. Let the Sauce rest until the Pasta is done cooking
