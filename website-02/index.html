HOW TO MAKE THE BEST LASAGNA EVER



Step 1 - Gather Your Materials:

  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 2 Bell Peppers
  • 1 onion
  • 1 Jar of Tomato and Basil Sauce
  • 3 Tomatoes
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 Pack of Lasagna Noodles
  • Oil
  • 1 Large Pot
  • 2 Large Pans
  • 1 Spatula
  • 1 Knife
  • 1 Cutting Board
  • Salt
  • Drainer

Step 2 - Create the Sauce:

  1. Add Oil to the pan and heat it up for 2 minutes
  2. Add Ground Beef to the Pan and crush it into small pieces with the spatula
  3. Cook the Ground Beef for 5 minutes, seasoning the beef however you's like
  4. While the Beef is cooking, Cut up the vegetables into small pieces
  5. One the Beef has cooked for 5 minutes, add the vegetables to the Pan
  6. Add the tomatoes to the Pan and crush them into a paste with the spatula
  7. Cook the Ground Beef & Vegetables for 5-10 more minutes, stirring frequently
  8. Let the Sauce rest with the heat off until the Pasta is done cooking

Step 3 - Cook the Pasta:

  1. Heat up 6 quarts of water in a pot untill it boils
  2. Add salt to the Pot
  3. Add the Lasagna noodles to the pot
  4. Boil the lasagna noodles in the pot for 10-14 minutes
  5. Drain the water from the pot

via GIPHY


Step 4 - Assemble and bake the Lasagna:

  1. Preheat an Oven to 375 Degrees ℉
  2. Lay out a layer of the noodles on a large pan
  3. Layer a cup of the Pasta Sauce on top of the noodles
  4. Layer all three cheeses over the Pasta Sauce
  5. Repeat steps 1-3 of this list until you have used up all the ingredients
  6. Put a layer of tinfoil over the lasagna and bake it for 30-40 minutes
  7. Remove the tinfoil and bake the lasagna for 5-10 minutes
  8. Remove the lasagna from the oven and let it cool
  9. Enjoy!
