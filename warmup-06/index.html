BRANDING



This is Chipotle's logo.

STOREFRONT



This is what a typical Chipotle store looks like.
The building is rectangle-shaped with white on top,
the logo on the front, and glass on the bottom.
Some buildings have a brick exterior on either side of the white and glass,
although these are typically reserved for mall restaraunts.
